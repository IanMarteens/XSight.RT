Materials: Plastic

Most plastic materials, in real life, are made of a transparent substrate holding small pigmented particles. The color of the material depends on these tiny particles, but specular reflection takes place in the transparent substrate.

Syntax

  • Plastic(color)
  • Plastic(color, reflection)
  • Plastic(color, reflection, phongAmount, phongSize)
