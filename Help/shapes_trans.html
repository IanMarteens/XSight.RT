Shapes: Transforms

Simple and complex shapes can be used along with a transform operation to create more complex shapes. These are the transform operations implemented by XSight RT:

  • Translate
  • Rotate
  • Scale
  • Dress (changing materials)
  • Shadowless
  • Repeaters
