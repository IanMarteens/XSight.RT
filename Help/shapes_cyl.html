XSight RT

Shapes: cylinder

See also

XSight RT supports two basic and very similar surfaces of revolution (SOR): cones and cylinders.

Syntax

  • Cylinder(Bottom, Top, Radius, Material)
  • Cylinder(Bottom, Height, Radius, Material)
  • Cylinder(X, Y, Z, Height, Radius, Material)

XSight RT does not provide open cylinders. However, you can simulate them using the difference operator, by removing the inner part from a cylinder using another smaller one.
