Marble differs from plastic in its peculiar color pattern, which involves at least two colors.

Syntax

  • Marble(color1, color2)
  • Marble(color1, color2, scale)
  • Marble(color1, color2, scale, stripes, octaves)
  • Marble(color1, color2, scale, stripes, octaves, reflection)
  • Marble(color1, color2, scale, stripes, octaves, reflection, phongAmount, phongSize)
  • Marble(color1, color2, scale, stripes, octaves, reflection, phongAmount, phongSize, roughness)

Actually, both scale and stripes are scale factors, though they apply to different features. Stripes controls how many stripes are grouped in a linear unit element. For instance, when stripes is 0.25, stripes will be separated by four units intervals. On the other hand, scale refers to the size of the added distorsion.

marble(royalblue, rgb(44, 71, 39), 4, 0.55, 4, 0.0, 0.4, 3)
