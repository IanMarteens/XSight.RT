XSight RT

Shapes: surfaces

See also

XSight RT offers two surfaces: triangles, which are finite sized surfaces, and planes, which are infinite surfaces. Future versions will include other analytical surfaces:

  • Triangle
  • Plane

Triangles cannot be used as operands in CSG operations, except unions. However, you can use planes for all Constructive Solid Geometry primitives, since they are interpreted as hemispaces.
