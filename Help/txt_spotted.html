Spotted materials directly maps the 3D Perlin's noise function to a gradient between two colors.

Here you have another spotted effect:

Syntax

  • Spotted(color1, color2)
  • Spotted(color1, color2, scale, turbulence)
  • Spotted(color1, color2, scale, turbulence, reflection, phongAmount, phongSize)
spotted(royalblue, rgb(44, 71, 39), 4, 2, 0.0, 0.4, 3)

The Spots pigment provides the same functionality, and can be used with the MetalPigment material to simulate a metalic look.
