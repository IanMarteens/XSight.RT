XSight RT

An Overview of the XSight Ray Tracer

See also

The XSight Ray Tracer is a small experiment with the .NET Framework and C#, in order to evaluate and demonstrate how fast or slow would perform in this platform a CPU hungry application, such as a clasical ray tracer. We believe XSight RT proves that the .NET Framework can handle this kind of applications. Now, is up to you to make your own opinion...
