XSight RT

Shapes: triangles

See also

XSight RT offers only one finite surface: the triangle. Triangles are useful to model almost any surface or solid by surface subdivision.

Syntax

  • Triangle(point1, point2, point3, material)
  • Triangle(aX, aY, aZ, bX, bY, bZ, cX, cY, cZ, material)
