Shapes: quart

See also

Quarts, or quartic ellipsoids, are convex solids that resemble boxes with rounded edges. They are geometrically related to quartic cylinders.

Syntax

A quart is specified by its origin, or center, and each of its axes:

  • Quart(center, radius, material)
  • Quart(cx, cy, cz, radius, material)
  • Quart(cx, cy, cz, rx, ry, rz, material)

Primitive quarts are always aligned to the axes. If you need another orientation, you must apply the Rotate primitive to the quart. You can also apply a Scale operation to the rotated quart. Note, however, it doesn't make sense to scale a non-rotated quart, since the same effect could be achieved by modifying the size of its axes.
