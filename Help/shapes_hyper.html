Shapes: hyperboloid

See also

One-sheeted hyperboloids are non-convex solids obtained by rotating a hyperbola around the vertical axis.

Syntax

A hyperboloid is specified by its origin, or center, each of its three axes and the coordinates of its bottom and top:

  • Hyperboloid(center, axes, bottom, top, material)

Hyper is accepted as synonym of Hyperboloid.
